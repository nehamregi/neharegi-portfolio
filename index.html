About

Designs Built
With Intention.

I'm Neha Regi, and I create merchandise and brand identities for organizations, competitions, and dance teams that want designs that feel personal, polished, and easy to bring to life.

My goal is to make the design process simple: understand the vision, create pieces that match the event or team, and provide mockups that make it easier to picture the final product before printing.

Whether you already know exactly what you want or need help narrowing down the vibe, I can help turn the idea into a merch-ready design.
